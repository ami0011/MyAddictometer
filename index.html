Manage Time

Addicto meter for us is an honest attempt to fight the battle against time. We are making sincere efforts to make all of us realize that there is still a lot of time for us to change. We can take back control of our lives.

Reconnect with the Reality

We are working extremely hard to detoxify people from the addiction of screens and virtual world. The tide is against us but we are not ready to give up. With the help of all of you we can bring tangible element back in our lives. Let's skip these screens and invest on ourselves, our friends, family and loved ones.

Value yourself

We have forgotten that we are not mechanical but biological machines. Our fuel is not only physical but emotional and spiritual. We have lost ourselves and are constantly losing ourselves to the virtual reality of screens.
